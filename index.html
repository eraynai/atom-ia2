Elli Raynai is the founder and head content creator at Cinehackers. He has over 20 years of experience in writing, producing, and directing independent projects. In 2014, his passion for art and technology led him to explore the possibilities of creating cinematic experiences in virtual reality. To date he has directed, written, and produced two features, seven short films, and four VR experiences.

Work Experience

Elli has worked in various industries such as IT and VFX, but his passion lies in creating narrative stories through immersive technology such as VR.
